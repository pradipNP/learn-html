Steps to Bake a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add wet ingredients: eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let the cake cool before frosting.

Groceries to Buy

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
